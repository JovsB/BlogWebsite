A Day in My Life:

School, Family, and Responsibilities

I wasn’t able to join Pahampang 2025, but my days are still busy and meaningful. Every day is a mix of school, helping my family, and finding little moments of happiness.

Early Morning Hustle

I wake up at 6 AM to help at home before going to school. My family runs a small eatery and store in our barangay, so I help by going to the public market to buy ingredients and supplies.

It’s not easy, especially since I stay up late studying, but knowing I can help my parents even in small ways makes me happy.

Getting Ready for School

After the market, I quickly freshen up and get ready for school in 10-15 minutes. I don’t usually have time for breakfast, so I eat after my second class—what I call my “brunch.” It can be tough sometimes, especially when my friends and I have different schedules, but I just go with the flow.
